# Purpose

This document captures future ideas for the AI Safe Use Knowledge Base for UK Charities.

It exists to:

- preserve promising ideas
- avoid losing useful suggestions
- separate ideas from committed roadmap work
- prevent scope creep
- make prioritisation easier
- connect future opportunities to evidence and user need

An idea in this file is not automatically approved.

It should move to the Roadmap only when:

- the user need is clear
- the evidence is strong enough
- the maintenance burden is understood
- the idea supports the project mission
- the project has capacity to deliver it

# Product Ideas

## IDEA-001 — Interactive Before You Paste Tool

**Status:** Exploring  
**Priority:** High  
**Category:** Product

### Problem

Users may understand the static guidance but still struggle to classify a real scenario.

### Idea

Create an accessible interactive decision tree that asks:

- Is personal data involved?
- Is safeguarding involved?
- Is the information confidential?
- Is the tool approved?
- Can the data be reduced or anonymised?
- Could the output affect a person?

### Output

- Green
- Amber
- Red
- safer alternative
- next step
- related guidance

### Requirements

- text-only alternative
- no case-specific legal advice
- no collection of sensitive scenario details
- no storage of answers unless clearly disclosed
- mobile and keyboard accessibility

# Ideas to Avoid

The following should not be prioritised without strong evidence.

## Complex Member Community

Reason:

- high moderation burden
- safeguarding risk
- distracts from the knowledge base
- existing platforms may already serve this need

## Paid Certification at Launch

Reason:

- may imply authority not yet established
- increases governance and assurance burden
- risks commercialising too early

## Tool Rankings

Reason:

- oversimplifies context
- creates affiliate pressure
- becomes outdated quickly

## Automated Legal Advice

Reason:

- high risk
- inappropriate project scope
- difficult to govern safely

## Individual Case Assessment

Reason:

- safeguarding
- privacy
- legal risk
- cannot be handled through a public knowledge base

## Large Custom Platform Before Validation

Reason:

- expensive
- difficult to maintain
- may solve untested problems

# Idea Evaluation Criteria

Score each idea against:

## Mission Fit

Does it help charities use AI safely and confidently?

## User Need

Is there evidence that charities need it?

## Risk Reduction

Does it reduce meaningful risk?

## Practical Value

Will users take a clearer or safer action?

## Accessibility

Can it be used by non-technical and disabled users?

## Evidence

Can it be supported responsibly?

## Maintenance

Can the project keep it current?

## Complexity

Is the build proportionate?

## Independence

Could it create commercial bias?

## Scalability

Will it work as the project grows?

# Moving an Idea to the Roadmap

An idea can move to the Roadmap when:

- the problem is validated
- the audience is clear
- evidence exists
- risks are manageable
- ownership exists
- capacity exists
- dependencies are understood
- definition of done exists

Create a Project Decision Log entry for major ideas.

---

# Rejecting an Idea

Record:

- reason
- evidence
- risks
- whether it may be reconsidered
- review trigger

Do not delete rejected ideas if they are likely to return.
