# Templates

These templates are practical starting points. They do not replace legal, safeguarding, data-protection, employment or procurement advice.

## Operational Templates

| Template | Use it when | Owner |
| --- | --- | --- |
| One-page AI use proposal | Someone wants to start or change an AI use | Tool owner or manager |
| Tool and feature approval record | A charity approves a tool, plan, model, connector or agent | Digital, procurement or data owner |
| AI-use register | The charity needs visibility of approved and pilot uses | AI governance owner |
| DPIA screening form | A use may involve personal data or high-risk processing | Data-protection lead |
| Meeting transcription assessment | A meeting may be recorded, transcribed or summarised by AI | Meeting owner |
| Human-review record | AI output affects a person, public claim or important decision | Accountable reviewer |
| AI incident log | Something may have gone wrong | Incident owner |
| Supplier questionnaire | A vendor, agency or platform will provide AI capability | Procurement or tool owner |
| Trustee assurance dashboard | Trustees need proportionate oversight | Senior owner |
| Content verification checklist | AI output will be published or used externally | Editor or communications lead |

## Minimum Fields for an AI-Use Register

- use ID
- owner
- purpose
- tool approval reference
- data ceiling
- people affected
- human control
- DPIA, safeguarding, equality and security screens
- decision and conditions
- review trigger and date

## Template Rule

Keep templates short for small charities, but do not leave out ownership, data category, human review, incident route or review date.

## Key Template Fields

### One-Page AI Use Proposal

- purpose and expected benefit
- tool, plan, feature and model
- data categories and data ceiling
- people affected
- human review
- DPIA, safeguarding, equality and security screens
- supplier or connector checks
- incident route
- owner, decision and review date

### Tool and Feature Approval Record

- exact product, account plan, workspace and feature
- permitted users and permitted tasks
- prohibited data and prohibited tasks
- contract, DPA and official documentation links
- training, retention, deletion and connector settings
- evidence of admin settings
- change triggers
- retirement and offboarding route

### Meeting Transcription Assessment

- purpose and necessity
- lawful basis and likely sensitive topics
- participants and power imbalance
- notice, objection and alternative route
- exact tool and data flow
- access, sharing, retention and deletion
- accuracy owner
- relationship between transcript, summary and official minutes
- incident route

### AI Incident Log

- time detected and awareness time
- reporter and incident owner
- system, account, feature and connector
- data and people affected
- containment steps
- evidence preserved
- provider contact
- privacy, safeguarding, cyber, fraud and content assessments
- notifications and deadlines
- decision reasons
- remediation and closure

### Supplier Questionnaire

- controller, processor and subprocessor roles
- processing purposes
- training and product-improvement terms
- retention, deletion and backups
- human access by supplier staff
- security, logging and audit controls
- incident notice
- international transfers
- individual-rights support
- connector and agent permissions
- change notice, export and exit
