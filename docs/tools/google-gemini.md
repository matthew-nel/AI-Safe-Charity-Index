# Google Gemini

Google Gemini may be used as a personal app or through Google Workspace. These are not the same governance situation.

<div class="risk-card risk-green" markdown="1">

## Green Uses

- drafting from public information
- summarising non-sensitive Workspace material in an approved tenant
- producing internal templates with no personal data

</div>

<div class="risk-card risk-amber" markdown="1">

## Amber Uses

- Gmail, Drive or Meet integration
- meeting transcription and summaries
- confidential internal documents
- Workspace use where admin controls and retention need review

</div>

<div class="risk-card risk-red" markdown="1">

## Red Uses

- work data through a personal Gemini account
- broad Drive access without a permission audit
- beneficiary decisions based on Gemini output
- case or safeguarding records in an unapproved account

</div>
