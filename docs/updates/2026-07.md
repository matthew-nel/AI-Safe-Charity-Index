# July 2026 Research Update

This update converts the first research batch into draft public guidance and navigation.

The research pack reviewed UK charity AI safety, data protection, safeguarding, governance, fundraising and common AI tools.

## Added

- DPIA triage for AI
- Interim automated-decision and profiling guidance
- AI fundraising guidance
- Meeting notes and transcription guidance
- Tool approval guidance
- Agents and connectors guidance
- AI incident guidance
- Account-plan and data-control guidance
- Draft tool guides for ChatGPT, Claude, Google Gemini, Microsoft Copilot, Notion AI, Perplexity, Canva AI and Adobe Firefly

## Changed

- Before You Paste now uses a five-step pause: tool approval, data category, necessity, AI action and human review.
- AI and Data Protection now reflects the July 2026 DUAA position at a high level.
- Governance now highlights trustee accountability and minimum controls.
- FAQ and Glossary now include core AI governance terms and practical questions.

## Human Review Required

The following remain draft or interim pending named review:

- automated-decision legal wording
- special category and criminal-offence examples
- meeting transcription lawful-basis examples
- safeguarding and beneficiary-facing AI use
- recruitment and HR use cases
- donor profiling and soft opt-in scenarios
- provider suitability for personal-data processing
