# Suppliers, Transfers and Contracts

Do not approve an AI supplier from a product page alone. Approve the role, contract, data flow, controls and exact feature.

## Controller or Processor?

Before using an AI tool with personal data, decide whether the supplier is:

- a processor acting only on the charity's instructions
- a separate controller for some purposes
- a joint controller
- a subprocessor or model provider behind another service

This affects the contract, transparency wording, rights handling and accountability.

## Contract Checks

Check for:

- processing purposes and instructions
- training or product-improvement use
- retention, deletion and backups
- human access by supplier staff
- subprocessors and model providers
- international transfers and safeguards
- security controls
- incident notice
- help with rights requests, DPIAs and audits
- connector and agent permissions
- data export and exit
- notice of material changes

## Red Flags

- no clear organisation contract
- only consumer terms for charity work
- unclear training or retention terms
- no subprocessor list
- no deletion route
- no incident notification commitment
- broad connectors without admin controls
- supplier can change model/provider materially without notice
