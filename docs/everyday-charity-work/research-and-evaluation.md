# Research and Evaluation

AI can help search, screen, transcribe, code and summarise evidence. It can also fabricate sources, flatten minority views and make a method hard to reproduce.

## Minimum Method Record

Record:

- research question and protocol
- tool, model and date
- data submitted and approval route
- prompts or reproducible instructions where appropriate
- human screening or validation sample
- error and disagreement handling
- citation verification against original sources
- participant notice or consent where required
- limitations and material AI assistance in the output

## Citation Rule

Treat an AI citation as a lead, not proof. Open the original source and check that it exists, is authoritative, is current and supports the exact sentence.

## Do Not Use AI To

- invent research findings
- create fake quotes or case studies
- analyse raw identifiable responses in an unapproved tool
- replace ethical review
- conceal uncertainty
