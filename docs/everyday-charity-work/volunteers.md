# Volunteers

Volunteers should receive the same practical AI safety support as staff.

## Practical Rules

- do not require volunteers to use personal AI accounts for charity work
- give only the minimum data and connector access needed
- train volunteers on Before You Paste and incident reporting
- do not use AI alone to screen, discipline or remove a volunteer
- separate genuine skills or availability criteria from inferred personality or fit
- revoke AI access and connectors during offboarding
- provide accessible, non-AI alternatives where needed

## Higher-Risk Volunteer Data

Pause before using AI with:

- DBS-related or safeguarding information
- references
- health, disability or support needs
- complaints or conduct issues
- rota, attendance or performance records
