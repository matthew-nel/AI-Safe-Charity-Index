# Supplier Contracts and Procurement

Procure the exact AI service, plan, account, feature and configuration. A supplier's brand reputation is not enough.

## Supplier Questionnaire

Ask for:

- controller, processor and subprocessor roles
- processing purposes
- training and product-improvement terms
- retention, deletion and backups
- human review by supplier staff
- security controls and certifications
- incident notice and support
- subprocessors and model providers
- international transfers
- individual-rights support
- connector and agent permissions
- logging and audit controls
- SSO, MFA and role-based access
- accessibility and inclusion considerations
- IP, output and content-use terms
- change notice
- service continuity, export and exit

## Procurement Gates

Require specialist review for:

- personal, special category or criminal-offence data
- children, adults at risk or vulnerable groups
- write-access connectors or agents
- credentials, secrets or security-sensitive systems
- payment, procurement or fraud workflows
- large file repositories or enterprise search
- public deployment or beneficiary-facing AI
- unclear deletion, training, subprocessor or transfer terms

## Exit Plan

Before approving a tool, know how the charity will:

- remove users
- revoke connectors
- export required records
- delete prompts, files and outputs where possible
- preserve incident or audit evidence where required
- retire links from policies, training and templates
