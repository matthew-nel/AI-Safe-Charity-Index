# About the Creator

Hello, I'm **Matthew James Nel**.

I created the **AI Safe Use Knowledge Base for UK Charities** because I believe charities deserve practical, trustworthy guidance on artificial intelligence without needing a legal team, data protection specialist or large digital department.

This project combines my experience in digital strategy, user experience, content design, analytics and charity technology with a commitment to making complex topics understandable.

---

# Why I Started This Project

While working in the charity sector I saw the same questions come up repeatedly:

- Can we use ChatGPT?
- What can we safely upload?
- Do we need an AI policy?
- How does GDPR apply?
- What should trustees know?
- Where do we even begin?

Much of the available guidance was either too technical, too generic or written for large organisations.

I wanted to build something that small and medium-sized charities could actually use.

---

# Design Philosophy

The project follows a few simple beliefs:

- People come before technology.
- Plain English beats jargon.
- Evidence beats opinion.
- Good governance should be achievable.
- AI should support people, not replace them.
- Human judgement remains essential.
