## Purpose

Help UK charities use Google Gemini safely and proportionately.

Read alongside the Before You Paste Framework, GDPR Decision Rules, Safeguarding Guidance and AI Tool Evaluation Framework.

## Suitable Uses

- Website copy
- Newsletters
- Grant draft structure
- Meeting summaries
- Public report summaries
- Brainstorming
- Coding assistance
- Plain English rewrites

## Green

- Public information
- Fictional examples
- Published reports
- Generic templates
- Translation

## Amber

- Anonymised internal documents
- Internal planning
- Draft strategies
- Aggregated survey findings
- Connected Google Workspace content

Requires approved organisational use and human review.

## Red

Never upload:

- Safeguarding records
- Identifiable beneficiaries
- HR records
- Payroll
- Donor databases
- Passwords
- API keys
- Legal privilege
- Medical information

## Google Workspace

Review:

- account type
- admin controls
- permissions
- retention
- organisational policy

## Human Review

Always check:

- accuracy
- bias
- accessibility
- tone
- references
- organisation-specific details

## Governance

Define:

- approved accounts
- approved use cases
- prohibited data
- review requirements
- staff training

## Review Triggers

Review after:

- major Gemini releases
- Workspace AI updates
- privacy changes
- regulator guidance

## Success

Charities understand when Google Gemini is appropriate, what information should never be entered and why meaningful human oversight remains essential.
