Contact the project if you believe it has:

- published personal data

- published confidential material

---

# Safeguarding

Do not use this project contact page to report an active safeguarding concern.

If someone may be at risk:

- use the safeguarding procedure of the relevant organisation
- contact the organisation's safeguarding lead
- contact the appropriate statutory service
- contact emergency services where there is immediate danger

Do not send safeguarding case details to this project.

This knowledge base does not provide case-specific safeguarding advice.

---

# Emergencies

This project is not an emergency service.

For immediate danger, contact the appropriate emergency service.

Do not wait for a response from this project.
