## Purpose

Quarterly Assurance is the strategic quality review of the entire knowledge base.

Monthly reviews answer:
- What changed?

Quarterly assurance answers:
- Is the knowledge base still accurate?
- Is it still trusted?
- Is it still easy to understand?
- Are we solving the right problems?
